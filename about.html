About Skypoint

Mombasa's go-to wholesale supplier for Canon toners, copiers, printers & laptops.

Our Story

Rooted in Mombasa,
Built on Trust

Skypoint Toners / Copiers / Laptops started with a simple mission: give Mombasa's businesses access to genuine Kyocera products at wholesale prices — without the hassle of dealing with middlemen or unreliable stock.

Operating from our godown on Kwashibu Road, Mombasa, we serve print shops, corporate offices, resellers and individual buyers across the Coast region. Our shelves are always stocked — Canon toners, photocopiers running up to 37 ppm, all-in-one printers and competitively priced refurbished laptops.

Our 5.0 Google rating and over 9,600 combined Facebook followers speak to the loyalty we've earned through honest pricing, genuine products and fast, personal service — much of it through WhatsApp.
